About Ghana

Popularly know as "The Gateway to Africa" is a beautiful country located on the Gulf of Guinea, just north of the Equator. A country where history is preserved in colonial forts, and the future is written in the bustling tech hubs of Accra. She is the world's second-largest cocoa producer and a land rich in gold, but its truest wealth lies in its people.
